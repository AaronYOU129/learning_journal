## 🧠 Prompt Engineering
## Chapter 1. Guidelines
### Principle 1: Write Clear and Specific Instructions

1. Use delimiters to separate input:
   - """
   - ---
   - <tag>

2. Ask for structured output:
   - JSON
   - HTML

3. Ask the model to verify whether the output meets requirements.

4. Use few-shot prompting:
   - Provide input-output examples before asking a new question.

### Principle 2: Give the Model Time to Think

1. Specify the Steps to complete a task
   Step1
   Step2
   Step3
   

3. Instruct the model to work out their own solutions before rushing out a solution
